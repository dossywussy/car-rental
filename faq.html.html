Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about renting with DriveEase? We've got answers! If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How do I book a car?

You can book online through our website, via our mobile app, or by calling our reservations team. Simply select your dates, location, and vehicle type.

What are the rental requirements?

Renters must be at least 21 years old (25 for certain luxury vehicles), hold a valid driver's license, and provide a credit card for payment and security deposit. International renters may need additional documentation like a passport.

Are there mileage limits?

Most rentals include unlimited mileage, but some promotions or vehicle types may have limits. Check your booking confirmation for details.

What if I need to cancel or modify my reservation?

Cancellations are free up to 24 hours in advance. Modifications may incur a fee depending on the timing. Contact us directly for assistance.

What happens if the car breaks down?

Our 24/7 roadside assistance covers breakdowns, flat tires, and towing. Call the number on your rental agreement for immediate help.

Can I rent one-way?

Absolutely! We offer one-way rentals between our locations for added convenience, with applicable drop-off fees.
